I wasn't going to post this week. Then I realized the reason I wasn't going to post this week was exactly *why* I should post this week.

So here we are.

## What's Actually Been Happening

The past month has been one of those stretches where everything is technically fine but nothing feels fine. You know the type. You're shipping things, answering emails, showing up — but the internal state is static, not signal.

I've been in the middle of the biggest game project I've taken on solo. It's going. I'm three months in. The scope is right, the design is tight, I know what I'm building.

But.

There's a certain kind of creative exhaustion that comes from working alone, where you don't get the sanity checks you get from a team. Every decision is final until it isn't. Every creative problem is yours to solve from scratch.

I don't say this as a complaint. I chose this. I still choose it. But I think it's worth naming.

## What I've Been Playing

Because the algorithm loves this content and also because it's genuinely what's keeping me sane:

**Animal Well** — This game is immaculate. It's the kind of game you make when you've been making games for a decade and you finally have permission to make exactly the thing you want. I'm jealous of it in the way that makes you want to work harder.

**Balatro** — I've lost too many hours. It's fine. It's poker but actually good. You'll get it when you play it.

**Neon White** — Late to this. Embarrassingly late. Absolutely worth it.

## The Setup Right Now

My workspace has evolved. I'm currently running:

- Godot 4 for the main project
- Aseprite for all the pixel work
- Obsidian for design docs and notes (my entire brain is in there)
- A mechanical keyboard that my neighbors have filed a formal complaint about

The desk is cleaner than it's been in months. I think that's helping.

## What's Coming
